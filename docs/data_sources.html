Data sources

Ontario

  • Ontario's daily status updates: https://data.ontario.ca/dataset/status-of-covid-19-cases-in-ontario
  • Data for each confirmed case (updated less frequently): https://data.ontario.ca/en/dataset/confirmed-positive-cases-of-covid-19-in-ontario
  • Ontario has their own summary of the current status of cases here: https://www.ontario.ca/page/2019-novel-coronavirus

Canada

  • Total cases for Canada and other provinces: https://www.canada.ca/en/public-health/services/diseases/2019-novel-coronavirus-infection.html

The world

  • Our World in Data has been doing a great job compling data for all the countries in the world https://ourworldindata.org/coronavirus-source-data
  • Our World in Data also has the best interactive visualizations I have seen: https://ourworldindata.org/coronavirus
